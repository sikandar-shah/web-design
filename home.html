Welcome to PSL Directory

Pakistan Super League (Urdu: پاکستان سُپر لِیگ‎; PSL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league, founded on 9 September 2015 with five teams and now comprises six teams. Instead of operating as an association of independently owned teams, the league is a single entity in which each franchise is owned and controlled by investors. The PSL season runs between the months of February and March, with each team playing matches in double round robin format; the top four teams with the best record qualify for the playoffs and culminates in the championship game, the PSL Cup Final. The league is based out of the Pakistan Cricket Board office in Lahore.

Why Pakistan super League?

The cricket comes home mantra has been oft repeated over the past many years. Cricket in Paksitan had been facing lots of security problems and incidents like the attack on Sri Lankan team is a part of history which we have to live with. Everyone in Pakistan wanted cricket back home and PSL is a positive step towards that goal. Many international players also participate in PSL and with time international cricket will once again start in Pakistan and there would be no need for international venues. New players emerge in this format and its really a spectacle to watch. It also provides a way to scout good future players for Paksitan national team.
